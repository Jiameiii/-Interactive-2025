Hello, Let’s Create a Zoetrope Together!

🎥 Experience the Magic of Motion! 🎥

Welcome to our interactive space where creativity meets animation! Have you ever wondered how moving pictures came to life before digital screens?
The zoetrope is one of the earliest forms of animation, using spinning images to create the illusion of motion.
Now, it’s your turn to design, print, and build your own zoetrope—a hands-on journey into the magic of motion!

💭 Step 1: Design Your Animation 💭

Create a series of sequential images that will bring your zoetrope to life. You can:
Draw by hand 🖍️
Use digital tools 🎨
Download one of our pre-made templates

✂️ Step 2: Print & Cut ✂️

Once your animation strip is ready, print it on sturdy paper and cut it to size. The length of the strip should match the circumference of your zoetrope drum.

💽 Step 3: Build Your Zoetrope 💽

Assemble your zoetrope using our DIY template or a 3D-printed base. You'll need:
- A cylindrical drum (your animations, 6-8 max)
- 1/8” x 1” Zinc plated fender washers
- A sturdy base for spinning (Cd, or cut cardboard box into circle)
- Chopstick
